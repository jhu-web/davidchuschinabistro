About This Website

It may be hard to tell, but this website does much more than just help you choose a delicious dish from this cozy little restaurant. The website of this restaurant has served over 100,000 students all over the world, teaching them the skills of how to develop a website from scratch.

The development of this website was part of the cirriculum of 2 Coursera.org courses: HTML, CSS, and Javascript for Web Developers and Simple Page Web Applications with AngularJS.

Coursera is what is called a MOOC or Massive Open Online Courses. It works with universities and other organizations to make some of their courses available online, offering courses in subjects such as engineering, medicine, data science, among others.
All courses have an option to be taken for free.

As of November, 2016, each of these courses made it to #1 Top Rated Course (amoung over 2,000 available courses), serving more than 100,000 students all over the world.
